Developing Nayabazar

Apr 16, 2023

  Prasiddha Acharya

"Nayabazar" is located in Eastern part of Nepal, Ilam district. It is the Headquater of Maijogmai Rural Municipality. Nayabazar is quite developed and still developing, there is good provision for drinking water,schools, hospitals etc. But since a long period of time, there was no proper facility of roads in Nayabazar.


Road is the main factor of the place to be developed, Roads plays a vital role in the development of the place. In the past, people of Naya Bazaar didn"t have even proper facility of roads. People living here have to struggle a lot for road. In rainy season, The roads got break down, Landslide came into the road and various types of natural disaster occurs in the road which used to stop the road. So, the transportation facillity got fully stopped during rainy season. People have to walk many kilometers to get the proper road. Its 19 km from Nayabazar to Fikkal and 20 km from Nayabazar to Ilam, People have to walk these many kilometers to get pitch road.

In past, a lot of circumstances were being faced by the people of Nayabazar in the term of road, Government had also bring many programs to build the road, but every plans and programs got flopped, Sometimes, Contractor leaves the work and ran away with the money, sometimes budget get finished, sometimes, natural disaster occur & the construction of road gets stopped

But, this time the road from Fikkal to Nayabazar is being pitched rapidly. Now, the contractor (Khanal Constructions) was also good, the season was was also good and everything goes well. So, the road is being pitched rapidly & people of Nayabazar were getting a proper facility of road. The contractor (Khanal Constructions) is doing its work properly and rapidly before the fiscal year ends.

From the magh of 2079, Khanal Constructions started pitching the road. For the first period of time, it pitched the road from Fikkal to Pandamkhola & For the second period of time, it pitched the road from Nayabazar to Dokandada. The pitching process has been final in Fikkal to Pandamkhola section & Second layer pitching process is being done from Nayabazar to Dokandada section . Contractor is closing the road for a few days, pitches the road & agains opens the road. During the closhing of the road, vehicles have to a long road to go to fikkal. It"s quite difficult, but it"s being for a proper road

As for the contractor of Khanal Constructions, the pitching of the road will be completed within the end of fiscal year 2079/2080. After the fully completition of the pitching of the road from Fikkal to Nayabazar, People living in Nayabazar will get a lot of facilities. It will be easier for them to travel to cities like Fikkal, Ilam, Birtamode etc. It will save the time and cost of the people.
