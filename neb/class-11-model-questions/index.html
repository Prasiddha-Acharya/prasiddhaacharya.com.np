When will be Class 11 exams started?

National examination board (NEB) has provided all the rights of taking the examination of class 11 to respective schools, So the date when the class 11 examination will be started depends upon the schools. Most of the schools are planning to start the class 11 examination from 16 th of Jestha, 2080. But it may be different in context of your school. So, please check your school notice before confirming the examination date.
